Carlos and the Physics Notepad

There was a man called Carlos, a Spanish scientist exploring the laws of physics.
One day in his basement, he found an old smelling notepad with math questions.
The more he looked, the clearer it became: the army held the laws of physics!
Carlos took a boring 5-hour drive to the army base.
He asked about the laws of physics, but the army refused to answer.
He took a 1-day drive back home... stuck in traffic.
His house was destroyed by a nuclear facility. It smelled more like hotdogs than smoke.
The End
