# Archive Quest (HTML/CSS/JS)

Open `index.html` in a browser.

## How to play
1. **Step 1 (micro-game):** survive ~25 seconds and avoid the moving “facility”.
2. **Step 2 (Quiz):** answer the **10 Medium questions**.
3. **Step 3:** if you score **10/10**, you unlock the **corrupted “What feels archived” note**.

## Controls
- Move: **Arrow keys** or **A / D**

## Files
- `index.html`
- `style.css`
- `script.js`
